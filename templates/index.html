Loan eligibility prediction is a huge problem in the financial sector which has a significant potential in benefitting both lenders and borrowers. There are various loans a financial sector provides starting from education loan, home loan, car loan etc.

In general, lenders such as banks and other financial institutions receive a huge number of loan application daily. It takes a huge amount of time to consider each user profile and history to approve a loan and it might lead to human errors as well this shows a bad remark on the company. To make this process easier and less time consuming and as efficient as much as possible by minimizing human prone errors we will be building a machine learning model in evaluating the loan eligibility. This helps the financial institutions in improving their efficiency in the loan approval process and to decrease their operational costs.

Since we make the loan eligibility process easier and faster for the borrowers this makes a great impact on the customer satisfaction level. Furthermore, the model helps us in reducing the risk of giving a loan to potential borrowers who might default the loan, this helps in reducing the financial loss.

We used various Machine Learning Classification Models for exploring the data and by find tuning the parameters of the models we achieved an overall best accuarcy of 86% by using XGBOOSTER. And the models precision, recall and F1-scores are good when compared with the other classificaiton models.
